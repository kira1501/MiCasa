The objective of this project is to create a standalone system which will monitor your home and allow you to access images, videos and information about your home from a remote location.

features
1] getting a live footage of your home form any part of the world.
2] controlling your home features like doors, garage etc from a remote location.
3] allowing guest to have temporary access to your home using remote login feature
(temporary passcode which can be typed on the keypad)
4]face recognition or fingerprint authentication of registered users.
